Game Rules ▼

Housie, also known as Bingo or Tambola, is a game of chance played with numbers from 1 to 90. Here's how to play:

  • Draw Numbers: The caller (that's the left side of this screen) draws a random number from 1 to 90.
  • Mark Your Ticket: If the number called is on your ticket, you can click on it to mark it.
  • Claim a Win: When you complete a winning pattern, press the **"Claim a Win!"** button.
  • Winning Patterns: The game checks for the following patterns:
    • Early 5: The first player to mark any five numbers on their ticket.
    • Top Line: All numbers in the first row are marked.
    • Middle Line: All numbers in the second row are marked.
    • Bottom Line: All numbers in the third row are marked.
    • Full Housie: All 15 numbers on your ticket are marked.
